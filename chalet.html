Equipment & Facilities

The chalet has a pool, nordic bath, sauna, fireplace, and ski room.

Pool

  • See safety poster on site
  • Shower required before use

Nordic Bath

  • Shower required before use
  • Do not pour liquid (alcohol, shower gel, etc.)
  • Do not introduce objects that could obstruct the filters
Under 15s: Prohibited without adult supervision.

Sauna

  • Maximum 15 minutes per session
  • Maximum 2 ladles of water onto the stones per use
  • Equipment damage from misuse will be charged to the customer
Under 15s: Prohibited.

Fireplace

The chalet has an insert-style fireplace. To start:

  • Place kindling with a fire starter and 1 log
  • Close the insert window tightly
  • Open the draft all the way to start
  • Once lit, feed with logs (max 2 at a time) and reduce the draft by half (push back the small pull tabs)
